UX SKILLS

UX Research

Research is the backbone of any UX idea. By exploring past solutions, testing with users, and drawing inspiration from other fields, I’m able to understand the users, their problem and its factors thoroughly without bias.

User Empathy

Understanding the user comes from researching, speaking with, interacting with, and observing them. Ultimately the experience is for them, so I take what I’ve learned from them and interpret it according to UX principles and my experience.

Qualitative Interviewing

I let the interviewee guide the process, treating it as a conversation instead of an interrogation. I’ve found this makes people more comfortable, and they tend to express concerns and ideas beyond my predictions --ensuring the product fulfills their needs.
